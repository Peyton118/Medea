Books For Book Wanters

Books For Book Wanters, or BFBW, is a organization dedicated to giving books to people who want to read books. What we do is use money from donations to send books to any and all who ask for one, they are given a completely randomized book from a genre of their choice. All that is needed to receive a book from us is a location for us to send the book to and your first and last name, that's all. To begin push on the button below that says "order a book", however if you instead wish to donate to our cause push the button that says "donate now"
